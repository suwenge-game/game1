About the Game

Learn more about Wood Block Jam and its developer

Wood Block Jam is a puzzle game where every move matters. You're in for a treat if you love clever challenges wrapped in vibrant design. In this game, you must guide brightly-colored blocks through a crowded wooden grid to their matching exits.

Each block only moves in the direction it's pointing, so every move needs to be carefully thought out. Some paths are blocked while others are frozen. Some tiles twist around corners, while others won't budge until you make space.

It's like a mini escape room packed into every level, where your mind is the only way out. With hundreds of increasingly tricky stages and fresh mechanics introduced along the way, this isn't just a puzzle game; it's a full-on brain workout.
